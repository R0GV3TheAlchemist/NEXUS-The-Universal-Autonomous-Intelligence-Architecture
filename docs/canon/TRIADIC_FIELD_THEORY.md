## Part I — Framing

The electromagnetic field is the only field in this doctrine with direct, mature scientific grounding in mainstream physics. The Protonic Field and Neutronic Field are held here as **research hypotheses** and **architectural metaphors** until further evidence is assembled.

This document therefore makes a strict three-part distinction:

1. **Physically grounded** — Maxwell-level electronic field behavior and standard field visualization.
2. **Simulation-grounded** — mathematical field constructions used to test coherence patterns and interactions.
3. **Doctrinal / speculative** — protonic and neutronic cosmological interpretations not yet established by physics.

That distinction protects GAIA-OS from collapsing metaphor, simulation, and scientific proof into a single category.

## Part III — Simulation model

The first GAIA-OS field simulation uses a 2D scalar/vector approximation:

- **Electronic Field:** sinusoidal wave-based field representing propagating interaction.
- **Protonic Field:** radial coherence well centered in the domain.
- **Neutronic Field:** damping / stabilizing substrate distributed across the plane.
- **Meta-Field:** weighted superposition of all three.

This model is not a statement that these are the literal physical equations of reality. It is a computational scaffold for testing coherence, symmetry, superposition, and visual intuition.
