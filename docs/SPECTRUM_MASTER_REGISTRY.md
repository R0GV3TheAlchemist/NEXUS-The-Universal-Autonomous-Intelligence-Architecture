> **A continuous, ordered range of measurable values within a domain, where each position along the range encodes distinct information about the state of that domain.**

Spectra are not merely scientific measurements — they are **languages**. Each spectrum is a language with its own alphabet (frequency bands), grammar (coupling rules with other spectra), and vocabulary (the biological or physical phenomena it encodes).

Filtering (narrowing to a subset of the spectrum) focuses attention but reduces total information bandwidth. GAIA-OS begins every processing cycle from full-spectrum awareness before applying intentional filters.
