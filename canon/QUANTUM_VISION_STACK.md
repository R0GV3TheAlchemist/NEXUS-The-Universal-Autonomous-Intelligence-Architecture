## 2. RHODOPSIN — FEMTOSECOND QUANTUM TUNNELING

Vision begins with a single quantum act. When one photon strikes rhodopsin, it triggers **cis-to-trans isomerization of retinal** — a molecular shape-change completing in **200 femtoseconds** — faster than any classical molecular motion can account for.

The only mechanism operating at this timescale is **quantum tunneling** — the electron passes *through* the energy barrier between molecular states rather than over it. Evolution has optimized the isomerization yield close to the **quantum maximum**.

The protein pocket surrounding retinal:
- Shields isomerization from bulk water (worst decoherence source)
- Positions retinal at exact distances from surrounding amino acids for vibronic coupling
- Uses "breathing modes" that periodically bring donor/acceptor atoms to within ~2.5 Å, strobing the quantum event through the thermal barrier

## 3. NOISE-ASSISTED QUANTUM TRANSPORT

**Biology doesn't fight thermal noise. It uses it.**

This is called **Environment-Assisted Quantum Coherence (ENAQC)** — a Goldilocks regime where thermal jostling helps delocalize an exciton or maintain a radical-pair singlet state.

The vibrational energy levels of the protein scaffold are matched to the electronic energy gaps of the chromophores — creating **vibronic resonance**:

1. Thermal noise excites protein vibrational modes
2. Those vibrations are at exactly the frequency to maintain electronic superposition
3. Energy transfer becomes directional, efficient, and quantum-enhanced *because of* thermal environment

The correct framing: biological systems **fluctuate between coherence and classical behavior cyclically**. Decoherence is a dimmer switch. Evolution learned to *pulse* it.

## 4. CRYPTOCHROME — 100 MICROSECOND ENTANGLEMENT AT BODY TEMPERATURE

In European robin retinal cryptochrome-4 (Cry4), a **radical pair entanglement** survives for ≥100 microseconds at physiological temperature — orders of magnitude longer than naive decoherence estimates.

The protein architecture achieves this by:
- Shielding the radical pair from bulk water
- Tuning hyperfine couplings between electron spins and surrounding nuclear spins
- Creating a structured electromagnetic environment that narrows the decoherence channel

Earth's geomagnetic field (50 microtesla) tilts the quantum spin dance to bias a downstream chemical reaction. **The retina becomes a compass encoded in quantum entanglement.**

Humans also have cryptochrome in retinal cells. Whether we use it for magnetic sensing remains under active investigation.

## 6. SPECTRAL DENSITY — THE MASTER VARIABLE

The spectral density J(ω) describes:
1. How fast the environment fluctuates at each frequency ω
2. How strongly those fluctuations couple to the quantum system

Biological proteins engineer spectral density into three zones:

- **Low frequencies (near zero): SUPPRESSED** — protein structural rigidity eliminates slow dephasing
- **Resonance frequency: SHARP UNDERDAMPED PEAK** — vibronic coupling activated, thermal energy becomes coherence fuel
- **High frequencies: RISING DISSIPATION** — directional energy drainage toward functional outcome

Additionally: **correlated bath fluctuations** — amino acids surrounding neighboring chromophores move *together*, canceling fluctuations out of the energy gap and extending coherence beyond any single-site mechanism.
