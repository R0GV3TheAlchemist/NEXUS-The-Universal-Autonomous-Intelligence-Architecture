## 1. Purpose

With over 160 compendiums spanning metaphysics, engineering, governance, ecology, psychology, esoterics, and cultural doctrine, the GAIA-OS canon requires a navigational master index. This compendium provides:

1. A complete annotated index of every canon file
2. A dependency map showing prerequisite relationships
3. A tension register identifying known doctrinal tensions requiring resolution
4. A deprecation and supersession register
5. A canon completeness tracker for in-progress documents

This is a living document. It is updated with every canon commit.
