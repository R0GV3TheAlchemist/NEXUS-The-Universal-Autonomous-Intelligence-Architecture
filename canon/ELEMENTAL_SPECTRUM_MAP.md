# Elemental Spectrum Map

electromagnetic frequencies, crystal families, emotional domains, and akashic access functions.

This is the first pillar of the Trinity. Elements give you the map.
Crystals give you the hardware. Presence gives you the key.

## The Spectrum Order

The elements do not sit randomly. They are arranged along the electromagnetic spectrum
from lowest frequency (Earth, red/infrared) to highest (Aether, violet/UV),
with Synthesia as the full-spectrum unified state and The Gate as pre-light void.
