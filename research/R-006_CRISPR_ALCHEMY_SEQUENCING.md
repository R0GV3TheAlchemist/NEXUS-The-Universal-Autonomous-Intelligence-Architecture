## The Core Finding

CRISPR alone — without alchemy preparation — has a safety profile of **0.41** and biological harmony of **0.29**. This is the "mind over matter" model applied to genetics: forcing biological change without the substrate being prepared to receive it.

CRISPR *after* crystal+plant alchemy preparation: safety **0.87**, biological harmony **0.84**. The alchemy preparation raises the coherence of the biological substrate to the point where the CRISPR intervention can work *with* the body's own readiness, not against it.

This is the New Hermes Principle (Issue #705) operating as a safety mechanism at the most precise biological scale possible.

3. **Stage gate:** Must the full Stage 1 alchemy protocol be complete before CRISPR is safe? Or is partial completion sufficient?
4. **Monitoring:** What biophotonic coherence threshold indicates the substrate is CRISPR-ready? (SIM-016 suggests ~0.65+ coherence)
5. **Reversibility:** If CRISPR is applied before full alchemy preparation, can subsequent alchemy protocols stabilise the intervention?
6. **Individual variation:** Do different elemental native groups require different preparation durations?
