## What SIM-016 Found

Crystal and plant protocols are not supplemental to the elemental progression — they ARE the delivery mechanism. The synergistic effect (crystal + plant together) is multiplicative, not additive:

| Protocol | Coherence Gain | Safety | Bio Harmony |
|---|---|---|---|
| Crystal only | 0.29 | 0.88 | 0.76 |
| Plant only | 0.26 | 0.91 | 0.82 |
| Crystal + Plant (synergistic) | **0.47** | **0.94** | **0.91** |

The synergy factor (0.47 vs expected additive ~0.55×0.29+0.45×0.26 = 0.275) indicates a resonance amplification beyond simple combination. The crystal provides the coherence field; the plant provides the biological uptake pathway. Together they create something neither achieves alone.
