## What Needs Validating

SIM-016 produced three categories of findings, each requiring a distinct validation pathway:

### Category A — Crystal+Plant Coherence Lift (SIM-016 Finding 1)
**Claim:** Crystal+Plant synergistic protocols produce a 0.47 coherence gain vs 0.18 for synthetic pharmaceuticals
**Validation approach:**
- Controlled biophotonic measurement study (before/after crystal+plant protocol)
- Instrument: BIOPHOTON_09 or equivalent single-photon counting system
- N ≥ 100 per elemental group (400 total minimum)
- Blinded protocol — participants unaware of which crystal/plant pairing they receive
- Measurement: biophotonic emission coherence (spatial and temporal) at baseline, Stage 1 completion, Stage 2 entry

### Category B — Stage Transition Readiness (SIM-016 Elemental Groups)
**Claim:** With alchemy, Earth-native signs show 96.9% Stage 1→2 readiness; all groups show 100% from Stage 2 onward
**Validation approach:**
- Longitudinal cohort study — minimum 12 months per stage
- Biological readiness markers to be defined per ELEMENTAL_PROGRESSION_MAP.md stage transition criteria
- Correlation study: do the proposed readiness markers (HRV, biophotonic coherence, somatic reporting) predict stage transition outcomes?

### Category C — CRISPR + Alchemy Sequencing Safety (SIM-016 Finding 2)
**Claim:** CRISPR + Crystal/Plant prep: coherence 0.71, safety 0.87, harmony 0.84 — superior to CRISPR alone (safety 0.41)
**Validation approach:**
- This is the highest-stakes validation track. No human CRISPR application proceeds without:
  - Full IRB approval
  - Pre-clinical animal model validation
  - Phase I safety trial (N ≥ 20, healthy volunteers)
  - Independent ethics board review (C155 Threshold Five prerequisite)
- The alchemy preparation protocol must be validated in Category A before this track opens
