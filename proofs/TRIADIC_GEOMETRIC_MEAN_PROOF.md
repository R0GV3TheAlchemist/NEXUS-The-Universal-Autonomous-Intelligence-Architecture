## Why Invariance Holds

When all three pairwise scores in a triad are equal (equilateral), AM = GM exactly (AM-GM equality condition). The closure threshold sits at a region where the equilateral configuration is the reference case. As structure degrades, both AM and GM fall — GM falls slightly faster when scores become heterogeneous (AM ≥ GM always) — but since the threshold is calibrated against the same equilateral baseline, the topology of the closure map is preserved.

The laws are **structural properties of the pairwise coherence function and the W_ANGULAR=0.50 dominance**, not artifacts of arithmetic averaging. Switching aggregation methods shifts individual scores but does not change the location of closure boundaries.
